Bliki: Interrogatory LLM (2026-05-14)

Fowler describes the "Interrogatory LLM" technique, where an LLM interviews a human one question at a time to elicit and structure the rich context needed for complex AI tasks — such as feature design or specification review — rather than requiring the human to write it upfront.
