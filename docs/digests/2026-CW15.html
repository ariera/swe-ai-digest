Eight years of wanting, three months of building with AI (2026-04-05)

Willison highlights Lalit Maganti's essay on building syntaqlite — a production-quality SQLite parser and linter — after eight years of procrastination, completed in three months using Claude Code. Absurd In Production (2026-04-04)
