Several interlocking themes emerge across the articles. Agentic AI systems dominate the conversation, with multiple authors examining their security vulnerabilities (prompt injection, file exfiltration, insecure configurations), their effect on open-source maintainers (curl's vulnerability flood, SQLite's formal policy rejecting AI contributions), and the challenge of keeping agents safely contained. Underlying many pieces is a consistent call for deliberate, disciplined human judgment — not as resistance to AI, but as its necessary complement.
